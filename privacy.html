Privacy Policy

Dream-Chain · Last updated: April 16, 2026

Dream-Chain ("we", "our", or "us") is a Pi Network application that generates AI visualizations of user-submitted dream descriptions. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Dream-Chain, we may collect:

  • Pi Network username and UID — provided by the Pi SDK when you authenticate.
  • Dream descriptions — the text you submit to generate a visualization.
  • Payment data — payment IDs and transaction IDs processed through the Pi Network payment system. We do not store wallet addresses or private keys.
  • Usage data — likes and interactions within the community gallery, stored in our database.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you via Pi Network and display your username in the app.
  • To generate AI images from your dream descriptions using the Pollinations.ai service.
  • To process and verify Pi payments for the visualization service.
  • To display approved dream cards in the community gallery.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Pi Network — for authentication and payment processing.
  • Pollinations.ai — your dream description text is sent to generate an AI image. No personal identifiers are included.
  • Supabase — our database provider stores dream entries and gallery data.

4. Data Retention

Dream entries submitted to the community gallery are stored until you delete them or request removal. You can delete your own dreams at any time within the app.

5. Children's Privacy

Dream-Chain is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us. We will remove your dream entries and associated data within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.

8. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact us at: sdandza@gmail.com
